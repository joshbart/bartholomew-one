The Bartholomews

Joshua and Kristi Bartholomew live together in a quiet neighborhood in North Fargo. They are active parishoners of the Cathedral of Saint Mary, helping in ministries such as the Cathedral choir. They also enjoy spending time with family in the area, especially during football season and over holidays. The Bartholomews are devoted fans of Minnesota's professional teams; especially the Vikings, the Twins, and the Loons.

Who we are

Joshua works as a Cyber Security Analyst at RealTruck. He also serves in the North Dakota Air National Guard as a Cyber Transport Supervisor.

Kristi works as an Administrative Assistant in the Tribunal of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Fargo.
